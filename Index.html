Bikaner

Bikaner is one of the most beautiful and historically significant cities of Rajasthan. Bikaner tourism promises to offer you a host of amazing sights to see and activities to indulge in. Places such as Junagarh Fort, Karni Mata Temple Bikaner, parts of the Bikaner desert area are all popular places to explore. Our Bikaner travel guide aims to familiarize you with all the intricate nuances of the city encompassing a host of amazing sights to see and activities to indulge in. The undulating sand dunes of Bikaner will take you to a magical place, offering families, solo travelers, honeymooners and groups of friends an escape into a picturesque setting.

Western Desert

Desert safari in Bikaner is a bumpy ride which one should experience at least once in a lifetime.

Junagarh Fort

Junagarh Fort was built in the year 1593 by Raja Rai Singh. The fort structure consists of temples and palaces.

Gajner Palace

Gajner Palace is among the popular sightseeing attractions of Bikaner, situated on the bank of a lake. The castle was built by former ruler of the Bikaner province, Maharaja Ganga Singh Ji.

Lallgarh Palace

This magnificent palace was constructed by Maharaja Ganga Singh in the new 20th century. The palace has now been transformed into a hotel and thus is an excellent place to spend your holiday.
